Koryu Naginata-jutsu in Ireland is a newly emerging interest, with a number of different practitioners practising various styles. The hope would be to create a community of practitioners who can share knowledge, techniques, and training opportunities.

The term "Koryu" refers to traditional schools of Japanese martial arts that have documented lineages and practices dating back from the time of samurai. (Artistic license applied here, lots of further reading where necessary.)
