Naginata Ireland hopes to promote the practice of Naginata in Ireland. We aim to provide resources, training, and community support for enthusiasts and practitioners of this traditional Japanese martial art.

Naginata practice can be approached from two different aspects:

  • Atarashii Naginata - a modern sport that emphasizes competition and physical fitness.
  • Koryu Naginata-jutsu - the traditional martial art schools that focus on historical techniques and kata.

We welcome all levels of experience, from beginners to advanced practitioners, and encourage anyone interested in learning more about Naginata to get in touch.
